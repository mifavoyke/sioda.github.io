OUR FOUNDER

Emma Walsh

In the heart of Dublin's creative quarter, Emma Walsh‚Äôs journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to create fashion that honours Ireland's rich textile heritage while embracing the future of sustainable design. Trained at Central Saint Martins and apprenticed under master weavers in Donegal, Emma's approach marries traditional craft with contemporary innovation.
